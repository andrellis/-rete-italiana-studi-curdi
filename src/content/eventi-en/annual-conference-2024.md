# Annual Conference 2024: "The Future of Kurdish Studies in Italy"

The Italian Network of Kurdish Studies is pleased to announce its **Annual Conference 2024**, which will be held on **March 15–16, 2024** at the University of Milan.

## Conference Program

### Day 1 – March 15, 2024

**9:00–9:30** – Registration and welcome coffee

**9:30–10:00** – Opening remarks
- Greetings from the Rector of the University of Milan
- Introduction by the President of the Italian Network of Kurdish Studies

**10:00–11:30** – Plenary session: “The State of Kurdish Studies in Italy”
- Speaker: Prof. Marco Bianchi (University of Bologna)
- Discussant: Dr. Sara Rossi (University of Turin)

**11:30–11:45** – Coffee break

**11:45–13:15** – Panel: “Contemporary Kurdish Literature”
- Chair: Prof. Anna Verdi
- Presentations by scholars and translators

**13:15–14:30** – Lunch

**14:30–16:00** – Workshop: “Research Methods in Kurdish Studies”
- Coordinator: Dr. Luca Neri

**16:00–16:15** – Coffee break

**16:15–17:45** – PhD thesis presentations
- Parallel sessions for doctoral students

### Day 2 – March 16, 2024

**9:00–10:30** – Panel: “Kurdish Politics and Society”
- Chair: Prof. Giuseppe Bianco
- Focus on recent developments in Kurdistan

**10:30–10:45** – Coffee break

**10:45–12:15** – Roundtable: “International Collaborations”
- Participants: European and Kurdish partner universities

**12:15–13:30** – Lunch

**13:30–15:00** – Workshop: “Digital Resources for Kurdish Studies”
- Presentation of databases and online tools

**15:00–15:15** – Coffee break

**15:15–16:45** – Closing session: “Future Perspectives”
- Open discussion with all participants

**16:45–17:00** – Conclusions and next steps

## Practical Information

### Registration
Participation is **free of charge**, but online registration is required by **March 1, 2024**.

### Accommodation
For participants coming from outside Milan, we have arranged special rates at hotels near the university.

### Certificates
All participants will receive a certificate of attendance.

## Call for Papers

We welcome proposals for oral presentations and posters on all aspects of Kurdish studies. Proposals should be submitted by **February 15, 2024**.

### Thematic Areas
- Kurdish literature and linguistics
- History and archaeology
- Politics and society
- Art and culture
- Gender studies
- Migration and diaspora
