**16th SeSaMO Conference – University of Cagliari (3–5 October 2024)**  
Panel proposed by Nicola Degli Esposti (University of Padua) and Andrea Novellis (University of Milan).

The panel explores new directions in Kurdish studies in light of political, social, and cultural transformations over the last two decades. Topics include institutional change, gender and activism, cultural production, and the geopolitical tensions across Kurdish regions.

**Chair/Discussant:** Nicola Degli Esposti (University of Padua)  
**Participants:** Özlem Belçim Galip (University of Oxford), Kubra Sagir (EHESS), Farangis Ghaderi (University of Exeter), Majid Aziz (independent researcher), Andrea Novellis (University of Milan), Davide Grasso (University of Turin).
