**SeSaMO webinars on Research and the Middle East**  
A series designed to provide a regular space for dialogue, debate, and self‑reflection on Middle Eastern studies in Italy and beyond.

### Programme

- **Date & time:** 18 January 2024, 16:00  
- **Title:** The Kurdish Question and the Public Role of Academia: Challenges, Limits, and Opportunities  
- **Talks:**
  - Zilan Diyar (journalist) – *Between academia and resistance, toward an ethics of knowledge*  
  - Fabiana Cioni (independent researcher) – *Nomadic research with women in Bakur and Rojava*  
  - Francesco Ventura (University of Florence) – *Importing the Middle East, importing criminalisation?*  
  - Federico Venturini (University of Udine) – *For research on the Kurdish question free from ignorance and prejudice*  
- **Discussant:** Giuseppe Acconcia (University of Padua)
