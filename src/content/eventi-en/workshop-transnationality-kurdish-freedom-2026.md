An international workshop exploring the transnational nature of the Kurdish Freedom Movement and its post‑statist geographies, in dialogue with the diaspora and European host societies.

### Programme (summary)

- **09:00–09:30** Institutional greetings  
- **09:30–10:30** Keynote by **Pinar Dinç** (Lund University) – *Transboundary Ecologies of War*  
- **11:00–12:15** **Panel 1 – Kurdistan** (Chair: Panos Bourlessas)  
- **14:15–15:30** **Panel 2 – The World** (Chair: Veronica Buffon)  
- **16:30–17:30** Keynote by **Joost Jongerden** (Wageningen University) – *Space and Politics in the Kurdistan Freedom Movement*

An accompanying art exhibition by **Rojda Turul** will be on display during the workshop.
