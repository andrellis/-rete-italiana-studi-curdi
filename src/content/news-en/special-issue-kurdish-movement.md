The introduction to the *Partecipazione e Conflitto* (PACO) special issue 18(2) 2025, **“Exploring the Kurdish Movement: Power Relations, Historical Dynamics and Theoretical Perspectives,”** is now available.

Published on **15 July 2025**, the editorial lays out the special issue’s analytical framework, with a focus on:

- historically embedded repertoires of state coercion and colonial governance,
- the movement’s evolving ideological corpus (Democratic Modernity and Jineolojî),
- micro‑level processes of mobilisation, activist identity formation, and internal organisation.

The article offers a multi‑level reading of the Kurdish movement and sketches future research directions on post‑insurgency transitions, DDR and autonomy design, and post‑statist political theory.

**Reference**

Grasso, D., & Novellis, A. (2025). *Introduction - Exploring the Kurdish Movement: Power Relations, Historical Dynamics and Theoretical Perspectives*. PACO, 18(2), 384‑394.
